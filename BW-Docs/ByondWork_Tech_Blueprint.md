
# ByondWork – Complete Tech & Execution Blueprint

## Overview
ByondWork is a B2B2C, community-driven sports & wellness platform for corporate professionals, focused on post-work engagement through real-world activities.

---

## Product Goals
- Encourage healthy after-work engagement
- Enable corporate-led community formation
- Simplify sports & wellness discovery and booking
- Build strong offline-to-online professional networks

---

## Core Tech Philosophy
- Serverless-first
- No/low cost tooling
- High scalability
- Fast MVP execution
- Frontend-first validation

---

## Frontend (Mandatory)
- Next.js (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui
- Zustand (state management)
- TanStack Query (API & caching)

Hosting:
- Vercel (Free tier)

---

## Backend (MVP)
### Firebase Stack
- Firebase Authentication
- Firestore (NoSQL DB)
- Firebase Cloud Functions
- Firebase Storage
- Firebase Cloud Messaging

Why Firebase:
- Zero DevOps
- Free tier friendly
- Fast to market

---

## Authentication
### MVP
- Email + Password
- Google Sign-in
- Manual company & city capture

### Phase 2
- LinkedIn OAuth (post-traction)

---

## Core Features
- User onboarding
- Event creation & joining
- Facility discovery & booking
- Community groups
- Leaderboards
- Corporate admin controls

---

## Payments
### MVP
- No in-app payments
- Pay-at-venue / UPI offline

### Phase 2
- Razorpay or Stripe

---

## Analytics & Monitoring
- Google Analytics
- Firebase Analytics

---

## Development Timeline
### Phase 0 – Validation (2 weeks)
- Landing page
- Manual onboarding
- Pilot events

### Phase 1 – MVP (6–8 weeks)
- Auth & profiles
- Events & communities
- Booking logic
- Notifications
- Leaderboards

### Phase 2 – Corporate Expansion
- HR dashboards
- Internal tournaments
- Reporting

---

## Cost Summary (MVP)
- Hosting: Free
- Backend: Free
- Domain: ~₹1000/year

---

## What NOT to Build Initially
- Mobile apps
- AI recommendations
- Wearable integrations
- Complex analytics

---

## Strategic Note
Success depends more on on-ground execution and community density than technology complexity.
